Services

⚙️

Configuration Management

Automating the provisioning and configuration of your infrastructure and what runs on it, saves you time and effort.

Change management best practices provide an audit trail, reduce human error and help improve business continuity.

🚀

Continuous Deployment

Zero-touch tools will automatically ship your app from version control to your host, rolling back if things go wrong.

Taking the hassle out of the workflow means happier developers who will release earlier and more often.

🔐

Security Hardening

The web is a dangerous landscape that is always changing.

Hardening servers and applications against attack, implementing intrusion detection systems and performing penetration tests can help defend your services and improve compliance.

🚨

High Availability

Keeping things resilient at scale and in the face of unexpected hardware, software and network outages is challenging.

mig5 can help design and monitor your kit to automatically scale, self-heal or fail over seamlessly to stay running.

✍️

Communication

From compiling an ISMS, to internal knowledgebases, explaining how things work is perhaps the most valuable asset.

Some clients simply engage mig5 to help respond to tenders, standing out from the crowd with creative and lateral solutions to the stipulated requirements.

🐧

Open Source and Social Justice

mig5 draws from decades of experience using Linux and is an active contributor to many FOSS projects.

Passionate about equality, privacy, and freedom of information, mig5 is especially interested in how free software can help non-profits, civil liberty groups and marginalised communities be empowered to express themselves safely and independently.

About

mig5 System Administration is the sysadmin consultancy business run by Miguel Jacq, a French-Australian and Melbourne-based Linux engineer with 15 years of industry experience working with many international businesses and communities to run safer, more resilient and efficient services on the web.

Miguel is an active contributor to open source projects such as OnionShare. Outside of work, he is a multi-award-winning writer who enjoys food, wine, literature, film, language, and embracing the different cultures his mixed heritage has provided him access to.
